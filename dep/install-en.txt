Open wine control panel using `wine control` command

Double click Internet Settings icon, then click "Content" tab, then click "Certificates" button

Click "Trusted Root Certification Authorities" tab and click "Import"

Click "Next"

Click "Browse.." then click Datenshi certificate that come with this app

Click "Next"

Choose "Automatically select certificate store"

Click "Next"

Click "Finish"

You'll get "The import was successful" message, and the import is done
